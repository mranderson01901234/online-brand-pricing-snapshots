## Issue body

**Dataset name:** Online Brand Pricing Snapshots

**Link:** https://github.com/mranderson01901234/online-brand-pricing-snapshots

**Category suggestion:** Economics / E-Commerce

**Description:**

Weekly snapshots of pricing behavior from 500+ online brand storefronts. Each snapshot captures current prices, original/comparison prices, discount percentages, and category context using a stable, versioned schema.

Designed for pricing analysis, market research, and academic work on e-commerce pricing dynamics. MIT licensed.

**What makes it useful:**
- Consistent schema across all snapshots (historical comparability)
- Behavioral focus (discount patterns, not inventory)
- Weekly cadence (captures trends without noise)
- Clear methodology documentation

**Format:** CSV

**License:** MIT
